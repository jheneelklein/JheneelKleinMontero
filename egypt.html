How to Visit the Pyramids in Egypt

A complete guide to exploring the ancient Pyramids of Giza and uncovering Egypt's rich history.

Introduction

The Pyramids of Giza are one of the most iconic historical landmarks in the world. In this guide, we will walk you through everything you need to know to visit the Pyramids, from the best times to visit, ticket prices, and how to make the most of your trip.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit the Pyramids is during the cooler months, from October to April. The summer months (May to September) can get very hot, especially during midday.

How to Get There

The Pyramids of Giza are located just outside Cairo, Egypt's capital city. You can reach the site by car, taxi, or public transportation. Many tour operators offer guided trips to the Pyramids, which may include transportation from your hotel.

Ticket Information

Tickets for the Pyramids complex vary depending on the attractions you wish to visit. The entrance fee includes access to the main Pyramids, but additional tickets are required to enter the Great Pyramid, the Solar Boat Museum, or to take a camel ride.

Tips for Visiting the Pyramids

  • Arrive early to avoid crowds and the midday heat.
  • Wear comfortable shoes as you'll be walking a lot.
  • Bring plenty of water and sunscreen.
  • Consider hiring a local guide to learn more about the history of the site.

Nearby Attractions

While you're in the area, don't miss out on other incredible attractions such as the Sphinx, the Egyptian Museum in Cairo, and the historic Khan El Khalili Bazaar. You can also explore the ancient necropolis of Saqqara and the pyramid of Djoser.
