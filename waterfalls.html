Waterfalls in Asia

Vietnam • Cao Bang

Ban Gioc Waterfall, Vietnam

Located on the border between Vietnam and China, this stunning waterfall is one of the largest in Asia.

Ban Gioc Waterfall, Vietnam
Ban Gioc Waterfall is one of the most beautiful and unspoiled waterfalls in Asia, located in a lush, mountainous region of Vietnam.

Laos • Luang Prabang

Kuang Si Waterfall, Laos

Known for its stunning blue water, this multi-tiered waterfall is a popular destination in Laos.

Kuang Si Waterfall, Laos
Kuang Si is known for its picturesque turquoise pools and scenic surrounding nature, making it a must-visit in Luang Prabang.

India • Karnataka

Jog Falls, India

One of the highest waterfalls in India, Jog Falls is an impressive sight during the monsoon season.

Jog Falls, India
Jog Falls is a majestic waterfall, best visited during the rainy season when the water cascades down with incredible force.

Indonesia • East Java

Tumpak Sewa Waterfall, Indonesia

Known for its powerful twin falls, Tumpak Sewa is one of the most stunning waterfalls in Indonesia.

Tumpak Sewa Waterfall, Indonesia
Tumpak Sewa Waterfall features two magnificent waterfalls cascading together, creating an awe-inspiring view in East Java.

Thailand • Kanchanaburi

Erawan Waterfall, Thailand

Erawan is a stunning seven-tiered waterfall, famous for its emerald green pools located in the Erawan National Park.

Erawan Waterfall, Thailand
Erawan Waterfall, known for its seven tiers and emerald pools, is a popular spot for tourists and nature lovers in Thailand.

India • Meghalaya

Nohkalikai Waterfall, India

Located in the northeastern state of Meghalaya, Nohkalikai is one of the tallest waterfalls in India.

Nohkalikai Waterfall, India
Nohkalikai Waterfall is known for its stunning height and the scenic surroundings of the lush Khasi Hills in Meghalaya.

Tad Fane Waterfall, Laos

Sekumpul Waterfall, Indonesia
Sekumpul Waterfall is one of Bali's most impressive waterfalls, featuring several falls surrounded by lush tropical vegetation.

Sapa Waterfall, Vietnam
