Best Waterfalls in Indonesia

Explore the most stunning and hidden waterfalls across Indonesia.

Top Waterfalls to Visit in Indonesia

Tumpak Sewa Waterfall

Located in North Sumatra, this stunning waterfall features a two-tiered drop that creates an impressive view, making it a must-see for nature lovers.

Gitgit Waterfall

A picturesque waterfall located in Bali, known for its refreshing pools and serene atmosphere. Perfect for a relaxing getaway.

Sekumpul Waterfall

Considered one of the most beautiful waterfalls in Bali, Sekumpul Waterfall offers a stunning natural landscape with seven waterfalls in one location.

Hidden Gems: Lesser-Known Waterfalls

Air Terjun Jagasatru

Located in West Java, this hidden waterfall is a perfect destination for those seeking tranquility away from the crowds.

Cikaso Waterfall

A beautiful multi-tiered waterfall in Sukabumi, West Java. The surrounding lush greenery makes it a perfect spot for nature photography.

Curug Cimahi Waterfall

Located in Bandung, this waterfall is famous for its blue lights at night, making it a unique spot to visit both day and night.
